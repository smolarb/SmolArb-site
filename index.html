ClearStay - made for cleanersurfaces.

Why do clean glass surfaces never stay clean for long? ClearStay is designed to help glass and other smooth surfaces stay cleaner for longer.

ClearStay opens a new direction in surface treatment technology. Designed for easy installation on existing glass and other smooth surfaces, it helps keep them cleaner and clearer with less maintenance. With potential for use in homes, commercial spaces, vehicles, and specialized applications, ClearStay represents a smarter step forward for everyday surfaces.

Key Benefits

What ClearStay adds to the surface.

Simple upgrade: improve the glass or smooth surface you already have without replacing it.

Low-power operation: designed to work with very little electricity in everyday use.

Less routine upkeep: reduce the need for frequent washing and repeated attention.

Better long-term value: lower cleaning effort and reduce maintenance costs over time.

What It Is

ClearStay is a surface film made for existing glass and other smooth surfaces.

It helps reduce water marks, dirt buildup, and repeated cleaning, so surfaces can look better for longer.

It is easy to add to existing surfaces, so people can improve what they already have instead of replacing it.

It can be used in everyday life, from homes and commercial spaces to vehicles and other smooth surface objects.

Why It Can Be Better

Why ClearStay can be more convenient and more cost-effective.

Compared with constant cleaning: it can reduce daily effort and help surfaces look better between cleanings.

Why this matters: less time spent cleaning, lower maintenance effort, and a more practical way to upgrade existing surfaces.

Compared with basic films: ClearStay is designed to improve how the surface performs, not just add another layer on top.

Compared with replacing glass: it can be applied to existing surfaces, which may make improvement easier, faster, and more affordable.

How it works

After the film is applied to the glass, a small controller is installed in one corner of the surface, with several operating modes, including automatic mode. When water, dirt, or dust reaches the surface, the system activates an effect designed to help prevent buildup from lingering. It is made for long-term use, uses very little electricity, and helps keep surfaces cleaner for longer while reducing the cost of frequent cleaning.
