Why Now

The market is shifting toward surfaces that must do more.

  • Glass, transparent layers, and finished surfaces are increasingly expected to contribute more than aesthetics or basic protection.
  • End markets are rewarding materials that add function without sacrificing clarity, clean design, manufacturability, or system integration.
  • Early platform companies can capture disproportionate strategic value before integration standards are established and larger incumbents fully organize around the category.

SMOLARB is being built for a future in which surface layers are expected to deliver visibility control, environmental performance, and differentiated product value rather than remain passive components.
